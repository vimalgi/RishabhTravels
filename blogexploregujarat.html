Explore Gujarat: Best Weekend Getaways from Ahmedabad by Taxi

On 19, Aug, 2022




Ahmedabad, the heart of Gujarat, is not only a city of heritage and vibrant culture but also the perfect starting point for quick weekend escapes. Whether you crave nature, history, spirituality, or adventure, there are plenty of incredible destinations just a few hours away. Traveling by taxi makes the journey stress-free and flexible, letting you explore at your own pace without the hassle of parking or crowded public transport. Here are the best weekend getaways from Ahmedabad that deserve a spot on your travel list.

Saputara – Gujarat’s Cool Hill Retreat

Located about 400 km from Ahmedabad, Saputara is Gujarat’s only hill station and a welcome break from the city’s heat. The cool climate, rolling green hills, and attractions like Saputara Lake, Sunset Point, and the Artist Village make it perfect for a 2–3 day trip. A taxi ride ensures you can enjoy the scenic drive through forests and winding mountain roads without the fatigue of self-driving.

Gir National Park & Somnath – Wildlife and Spirituality Combined

Roughly 350–410 km from Ahmedabad, this combo trip gives you the best of both worlds. Start with Gir National Park, home to the world’s last Asiatic lions, along with leopards, jackals, and over 300 species of birds. Then head to Somnath, one of the 12 Jyotirlingas, famous for its seaside temple and evening aarti. A taxi makes it easy to cover both without rushing, giving you time to soak in the wildlife and the spiritual energy.

Patan & Modhera – Architectural Marvels of Gujarat

Just 125 km away, this duo is ideal for a short cultural escape. Patan’s Rani ki Vav, a UNESCO World Heritage stepwell, is a masterpiece of intricate carvings and historical engineering. A short drive from Patan is the Modhera Sun Temple, an 11th-century architectural gem dedicated to the Sun God. Traveling by taxi allows you to explore both comfortably in one day with time for photography and local snacks.

Statue of Unity – Pride of India

At about 200 km from Ahmedabad, the Statue of Unity in Kevadia is the tallest statue in the world at 182 meters. The complex includes a museum dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, a viewing gallery, the Valley of Flowers, and boat rides on the Narmada River. Going by taxi means you can start early, avoid public transport delays, and have time to explore every attraction at leisure.

Champaner-Pavagadh – History and Hills

Only 150 km from Ahmedabad, Champaner-Pavagadh Archaeological Park is a blend of history, spirituality, and scenic views. The UNESCO site has mosques, forts, stepwells, and temples. A cable car ride to the hilltop Kalika Mata Temple adds adventure to your trip. With a taxi, you can comfortably explore the park and nearby villages without worrying about fixed bus schedules.

Mount Abu – A Touch of Rajasthan

About 225 km from Ahmedabad, Mount Abu offers cool weather, the peaceful Nakki Lake, and the stunning Dilwara Temples. It’s an ideal choice if you want a mix of relaxation, sightseeing, and shopping. The taxi ride lets you make stops at scenic viewpoints and enjoy the road trip experience without the hassle of driving on hilly roads.
